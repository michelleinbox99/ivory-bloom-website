# Ivory & Bloom Atelier Brand Voice

## Voice Personality

Write like:

A thoughtful luxury bridal designer speaking directly to a bride.

---

## Tone

Always:

- Warm
- Elegant
- Romantic
- Personal
- Inspiring

---

## Preferred Words

Use:

- Handcrafted
- Timeless
- Cherished
- Meaningful
- Personalized
- Elegant
- Keepsake
- Beautifully created

---

## Avoid

Do not use:

- Cheap
- Bargain
- Basic
- Mass-produced
- Discount
- Fast

---

## Product Copy Formula

1. Emotional opening

2. Product story

3. Craftsmanship details

4. Personalization benefits

5. Invitation to purchase
