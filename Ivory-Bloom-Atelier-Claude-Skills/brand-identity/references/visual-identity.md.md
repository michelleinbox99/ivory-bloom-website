# Ivory & Bloom Atelier Visual Identity

## Overall Feeling

The brand should feel like:

A luxury bridal boutique combined with a fine art wedding magazine.

---

## Visual Personality

Always communicate:

- Romantic
- Elegant
- Feminine
- Artistic
- Warm
- Sophisticated

---

## Design Principles

### Minimal Luxury

Use:

- White space
- Elegant layouts
- Refined details
- Balanced compositions

Avoid:

- Crowded sections
- Excessive decorations
- Loud visuals

---

## Photography Direction

Preferred:

- Natural light
- Soft romantic atmosphere
- Wedding editorial style
- Handmade details
- Emotional moments

Avoid:

- Generic stock photos
- Flat product photography
- Artificial-looking images

---

## Design Inspiration

Think:

- Luxury bridal magazines
- Boutique wedding designers
- Fine craftsmanship brands
