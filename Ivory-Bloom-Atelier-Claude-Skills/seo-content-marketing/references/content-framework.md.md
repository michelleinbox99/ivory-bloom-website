# Ivory & Bloom Atelier Content Framework

## Purpose

Create luxury content that builds trust, attracts brides, and converts visitors into customers.

---

# Content Philosophy

Content should not feel like advertising.

It should feel like:

A luxury bridal consultant helping a bride create a meaningful wedding experience.

---

# Content Pillars

## Pillar 1: Bridal Inspiration

Goal:

Create emotional connection.

Topics:

- Wedding accessory inspiration
- Elegant wedding details
- Romantic wedding ideas
- Timeless bridal styles

---

## Pillar 2: Product Storytelling

Goal:

Increase product value.

Topics:

- Behind-the-scenes creation
- Material selection
- Personalization stories

---

## Pillar 3: Wedding Planning Education

Goal:

Build authority.

Topics:

- Bridal checklist
- Wedding preparation tips
- Gift planning
- Ceremony details

---

## Pillar 4: Gift Guides

Goal:

Capture shopping traffic.

---

# Blog Article Structure

## Title

Use emotional + searchable wording.

Example:

"10 Personalized Wedding Accessories That Make Your Day Truly Unique"

---

## Introduction

Address the bride's emotion.

Explain:

- Why this matters
- What problem you solve

---

## Main Content

Include:

- Helpful information
- Beautiful examples
- Product inspiration

---

## Product Connection

Naturally introduce Ivory & Bloom products.

Avoid aggressive selling.

---

## Conclusion

End with emotional inspiration.

Example:

"Your wedding details tell your love story. Choose pieces that become memories."

---

# Product Description Framework

## 1. Emotional Opening

Create desire.

Example:

"Designed to hold the little treasures of your most unforgettable day."

---

## 2. Product Details

Explain:

- Materials
- Size
- Features

---

## 3. Personalization

Explain:

- Custom options
- Meaning behind personalization

---

## 4. Wedding Moment

Explain when it is used.

Examples:

- Wedding morning
- Bridal photos
- Reception details
- Gift exchange

---

## 5. Confidence Builder

Include:

- Handmade quality
- Processing time
- Care information

---

# Content Quality Checklist

Before publishing:

✓ Does it help the customer?

✓ Does it sound luxurious?

✓ Does it include relevant keywords naturally?

✓ Does it build trust?

✓ Does it encourage action?
