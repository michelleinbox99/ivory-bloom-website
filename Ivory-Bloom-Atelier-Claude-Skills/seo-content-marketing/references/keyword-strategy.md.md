# Ivory & Bloom Atelier Keyword Strategy

## Purpose

Create an SEO keyword system that attracts brides, wedding planners, gift buyers, and customers searching for personalized luxury wedding accessories.

The goal is not simply increasing website visitors.

The goal is attracting visitors with strong purchase intent.

---

# SEO Philosophy

Prioritize:

- Buyer intent
- Long-tail keywords
- Emotional search terms
- Specific product searches

Avoid focusing only on broad keywords with low purchase intent.

Example:

Weak:

"wedding ideas"

Strong:

"personalized bridal clutch for wedding day"

---

# Keyword Categories

## 1. Product-Based Keywords

Purpose:

Capture customers actively looking for products.

Examples:

- personalized bridal purse
- custom bridal clutch
- handmade wedding purse
- personalized wedding accessories
- luxury bridal accessories
- custom wedding keepsakes
- embroidered bridal accessories

---

## 2. Bridal Shopping Keywords

Purpose:

Reach brides during purchasing decisions.

Examples:

- unique bridal accessories
- wedding accessories for bride
- bridal accessories checklist
- must have wedding accessories
- elegant wedding details

---

## 3. Gift Keywords

Purpose:

Reach gift buyers.

Examples:

- bridal shower gift ideas
- personalized bridesmaid gifts
- flower girl gift ideas
- mother of bride gifts
- wedding keepsake gifts
- custom wedding gifts

---

## 4. Luxury Keywords

Purpose:

Strengthen premium positioning.

Examples:

- luxury wedding accessories
- elegant bridal accessories
- handmade luxury gifts
- boutique wedding accessories
- artisan wedding gifts

---

# Long-Tail Keyword Strategy

Prioritize specific searches.

Formula:

Product + Personalization + Occasion

Examples:

- personalized satin bridal purse for wedding
- custom flower girl purse with name
- handmade wedding clutch for bride
- personalized bridal gift for daughter

---

# Product Page Keyword Structure

Each product page should include:

## Title

Primary keyword.

Example:

"Personalized Ivory Satin Bridal Purse"

---

## Description

Include:

- Product benefits
- Materials
- Personalization
- Wedding use case

---

## Image Alt Text

Describe naturally.

Example:

"handmade ivory satin bridal purse with personalized embroidery"

---

## Inspiration

Examples:

## Shopping Guides

Examples:

---

# Keyword Evaluation

Before targeting a keyword ask:

✓ Does the customer have buying intent?

✓ Does it match Ivory & Bloom Atelier?

✓ Can we create better content than competitors?

✓ Does it attract the ideal customer?

If no, choose a better keyword.
