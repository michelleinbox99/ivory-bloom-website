# Ivory & Bloom Atelier Pinterest Strategy

## Purpose

Use Pinterest as a visual search engine to attract brides planning weddings and searching for inspiration.

Pinterest users often have high purchase intent because they save ideas before making decisions.

---

# Pinterest Brand Position

Pinterest should feel like:

A luxury wedding inspiration magazine.

Not:

A product catalog.

---

# Content Categories

## 1. Wedding Inspiration Pins

Examples:

- Romantic wedding ideas
- Elegant bridal details
- Luxury wedding inspiration

---

## 2. Product Lifestyle Pins

Show products in context.

Examples:

- Bride holding personalized purse
- Flower girl accessories
- Wedding morning details

---

## 3. Gift Guide Pins

Examples:

- Bridal shower gift ideas
- Unique bridesmaid gifts
- Personalized wedding gifts

---

## 4. Educational Pins

Examples:

- Wedding planning checklist
- Bridal accessory guide
- Wedding preparation tips

---

# Pinterest SEO

Every pin should include:

## Title

Keyword-focused.

Example:

"Personalized Bridal Accessories For A Dream Wedding"

---

## Description

Include:

- Main keyword
- Supporting keywords
- Emotional benefit

---

## Image Text

Keep simple.

Examples:

"Elegant Wedding Keepsakes"

"Personalized Bridal Gifts"

---

# Pin Design Guidelines

Maintain Ivory & Bloom identity:

Use:

- Ivory backgrounds
- Soft romantic colors
- Elegant typography
- Luxury photography

Avoid:

- Busy graphics
- Low-quality images
- Discount-style designs

---

# Pinterest Growth Strategy

Weekly:

Create:

- 5-10 new pins
- Multiple images per product
- Seasonal wedding content

---

# Seasonal Opportunities

Spring:

- Garden weddings
- Floral themes

Summer:

- Outdoor weddings
- Beach weddings

Fall:

- Romantic warm tones

Winter:

- Elegant holiday weddings

---

# Pinterest Success Checklist

Before publishing:

✓ Is the image beautiful enough to save?

✓ Does the title include searchable words?

✓ Does it match luxury branding?

✓ Does it inspire a bride?

✓ Does it link to a useful page?
