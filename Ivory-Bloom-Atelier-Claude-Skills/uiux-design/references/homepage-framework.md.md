# Ivory & Bloom Atelier Homepage Framework

## Purpose

Create a homepage that emotionally connects with brides and guides them toward purchasing.

The homepage must answer:

1. Who are we?
2. Why are we special?
3. Why should customers trust us?
4. What should they do next?

---

# Section 1: Luxury Hero

## Goal

Create an immediate emotional connection.

Required:

- Beautiful bridal image
- Brand statement
- Primary CTA

---

## Recommended Structure

Headline:

"Handcrafted Details For Your Forever Moments"

Supporting text:

"Personalized bridal accessories created with love, artistry, and timeless elegance."

CTA:

"Explore Bridal Collection"

---

# Section 2: Brand Story

## Purpose

Build emotional connection.

Show:

- Handmade process
- Artist story
- Inspiration

Message:

Every piece is created to become part of a cherished memory.

---

# Section 3: Featured Collections

## Purpose

Help customers discover products.

Recommended categories:

- Bridal Accessories
- Flower Girl Gifts
- Personalized Keepsakes
- Wedding Gifts

---

# Section 4: Craftsmanship Section

## Purpose

Increase perceived value.

Highlight:

- Handmade details
- Quality materials
- Personalization process

---

# Section 5: Lifestyle Storytelling

Show products in use:

Examples:

- Bride preparing
- Wedding morning
- Flower girl moment
- Gift exchange

Customers should imagine themselves owning the product.

---

# Section 6: Customer Trust

Include:

- Reviews
- Testimonials
- Wedding photos
- Customer stories

Trust reduces hesitation.

---

# Section 7: Customization Experience

Explain:

"Made Especially For You"

Show:

- Personalization options
- Custom colors
- Custom details

---

# Section 8: Final Emotional CTA

End with inspiration.

Example:

"Create A Keepsake That Tells Your Wedding Story"

CTA:

"Start Designing Yours"

---

# Homepage Conversion Rules

The homepage should not only look beautiful.

It must:

- Build desire
- Build trust
- Guide action
- Increase purchase confidence
