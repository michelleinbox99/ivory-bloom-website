# Ivory & Bloom Atelier Mobile Design Guidelines

## Purpose

Create a luxury mobile shopping experience for brides browsing on phones.

Mobile users must experience the same elegance as desktop users.

---

# Mobile First Principles

Design for:

- One-handed browsing
- Fast loading
- Easy discovery
- Simple purchasing

---

# Mobile Header

Recommended:

- Logo centered or balanced
- Simple menu
- Shopping bag visible

Avoid:

- Large headers taking too much screen space
- Complex navigation

---

# Hero Section Mobile

Prioritize:

1. Beautiful image
2. Clear headline
3. Simple CTA

Avoid:

- Tiny text
- Too many buttons
- Heavy animations

---

# Product Browsing

Mobile product cards should include:

- Large images
- Clear names
- Easy scrolling

Use:

- Vertical layouts
- Touch-friendly spacing

---

# Product Page Mobile

Above the fold should include:

- Product image
- Product title
- Price
- Customization options
- Add-to-cart button

Do not force users to scroll too far before understanding the product.

---

# Forms & Customization

Make inputs:

- Large
- Easy to tap
- Clearly labeled

Avoid:

- Small fields
- Confusing options

---

# Mobile Performance

Prioritize:

- Optimized images
- Fast loading
- Minimal unnecessary scripts

---

# Mobile Luxury Checklist

Before approval:

✓ Does it feel premium?

✓ Is navigation effortless?

✓ Are buttons easy to tap?

✓ Can customers purchase quickly?

✓ Does the emotional story remain strong?

---

# Final Question

Would a bride enjoy browsing this website while planning her wedding from her phone?

If not, improve the experience.
