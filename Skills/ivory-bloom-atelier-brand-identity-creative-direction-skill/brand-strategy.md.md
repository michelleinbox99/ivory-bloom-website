# Ivory & Bloom Atelier Brand Strategy

## Brand Story

Ivory & Bloom Atelier exists to celebrate the small details that make weddings unforgettable.

Every handmade piece is created to become part of a meaningful moment.

---

# Mission

Create elegant personalized wedding accessories that transform ordinary details into treasured memories.

---

# Vision

Become a recognized luxury handmade bridal brand known for:

* Beautiful craftsmanship
* Emotional storytelling
* Personalized experiences

---

# Target Customer

## Primary Customer

The thoughtful bride who values:

* Meaning over mass production
* Beauty over convenience
* Personalization over ordinary products

---

# Customer Desire

She wants:

"Something unique that feels created especially for my wedding."

---

# Brand Differentiation

## Handmade Experience

Customers connect with the maker.

---

## Personalized Details

Every piece feels unique.

---

## Emotional Value

Products become memories.

---

## Premium Presentation

Every interaction feels special.

---

# Brand Position Statement

For brides who want meaningful wedding details, Ivory & Bloom Atelier creates handcrafted personalized accessories that transform wedding moments into timeless keepsakes.
