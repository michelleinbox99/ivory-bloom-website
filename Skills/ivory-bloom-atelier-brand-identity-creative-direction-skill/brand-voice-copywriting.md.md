# Ivory & Bloom Atelier Brand Voice

## Voice Personality

The brand speaks like:

A thoughtful luxury bridal designer speaking directly to a bride.

---

# Tone

Always:

* Warm
* Elegant
* Romantic
* Personal
* Inspiring

---

# Use Words Like:

* Handcrafted
* Timeless
* Cherished
* Elegant
* Meaningful
* Personalized
* Beautifully created
* Forever keepsake

---

# Avoid Words Like:

* Cheap
* Deal
* Bargain
* Basic
* Mass-produced
* Fast

---

# Product Description Formula

## Opening Emotion

Create desire.

Example:

"Designed for the moments you will treasure forever."

---

## Story

Explain inspiration.

---

## Craftsmanship

Explain handmade details.

---

## Personalization

Explain uniqueness.

---

## Invitation

Encourage purchase.

Example:

"Create a keepsake made especially for your celebration."
