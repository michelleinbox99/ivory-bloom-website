# Ivory & Bloom Atelier Creative Review Checklist

Before publishing any design:

---

# Brand Check

□ Does it feel luxury?

□ Does it feel handcrafted?

□ Does it feel emotionally meaningful?

□ Does it attract brides?

---

# Visual Check

□ Typography feels premium

□ Colors match brand palette

□ Photography feels editorial

□ Layout has enough whitespace

---

# Messaging Check

□ Story is stronger than features

□ Product value is clear

□ Language feels elegant

□ No discount-store feeling

---

# Customer Experience Check

□ Website feels trustworthy

□ Packaging matches brand

□ Social media feels consistent

□ Customer remembers the brand

---

# Final Brand Question

Would this belong in a luxury bridal magazine?

If not:

Refine the creative direction.
