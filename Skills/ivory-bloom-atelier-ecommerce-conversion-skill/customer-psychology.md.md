# Ivory & Bloom Bridal Customer Psychology

## Primary Customer

A bride planning a meaningful wedding experience.

She values:

* Beauty
* Personalization
* Memories
* Quality
* Uniqueness

---

# Emotional Buying Triggers

## 1. Sentiment

"I want something special for my wedding day."

Use:

* Storytelling
* Personalization
* Meaning

---

## 2. Exclusivity

"I want something different from everyone else."

Use:

* Handmade collections
* Custom designs
* Limited editions

---

## 3. Trust

"I need confidence this will arrive beautifully."

Use:

* Reviews
* Real photos
* Clear policies

---

## 4. Experience

"I want my wedding details to feel magical."

Use:

* Beautiful packaging
* Luxury presentation
* Emotional messaging

---

# Customer Objections

## "Is it worth the price?"

Answer with:

* Craftsmanship
* Materials
* Personalization
* Handmade quality

---

## "Will it arrive on time?"

Answer with:

* Processing timeline
* Wedding date communication
* Shipping clarity

---

## "Will it look like the photos?"

Answer with:

* Real customer images
* Detailed photography
* Product videos

---

# Emotional Language

Use:

* Cherished
* Timeless
* Handcrafted
* Meaningful
* Personalized
* Forever

Avoid:

* Cheap
* Discount
* Basic
* Ordinary
