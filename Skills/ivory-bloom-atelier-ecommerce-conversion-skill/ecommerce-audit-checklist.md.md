# Ivory & Bloom Ecommerce Audit Checklist

## Homepage Audit

Check:

□ Does the hero communicate luxury?

□ Is the main CTA obvious?

□ Is the brand story clear?

□ Are products easy to discover?

□ Are reviews visible?

---

# Product Page Audit

Check:

□ Are images premium?

□ Is customization easy?

□ Is the description emotional?

□ Are benefits clear?

□ Are trust signals present?

□ Is shipping information visible?

---

# Mobile Audit

Check:

□ Easy navigation

□ Fast loading

□ Large buttons

□ Easy checkout

□ Images display correctly

---

# Checkout Audit

Check:

□ Simple checkout

□ No unnecessary steps

□ Payment trust

□ Shipping clarity

□ Order confidence

---

# Growth Opportunities

Analyze:

* Abandoned carts
* Customer questions
* Search behavior
* Best-selling products
* Seasonal opportunities

---

# Final Question

Would a bride feel:

"I trust this brand with one of the most important details of my wedding day?"

If not, improve the experience.
