# Ivory & Bloom Atelier Brand Guidelines

## Brand Personality

The brand should feel:

* Romantic
* Elegant
* Feminine
* Artistic
* Warm
* Personal
* Timeless

Imagine:

A luxury bridal boutique mixed with a fine art studio.

---

## Customer Emotion

Every page should make customers feel:

"I found something beautiful and meaningful for my wedding."

---

## Photography Style

Preferred:

* Ultra-realistic luxury wedding photography
* Natural daylight
* Soft romantic atmosphere
* Handmade details
* Premium editorial style

Show:

* Brides
* Wedding ceremonies
* Flower girls
* Elegant tablescapes
* Close-up craftsmanship

Avoid:

* Generic stock photography
* Artificial-looking images
* Cheap ecommerce product shots

---

## Product Presentation

Products should appear:

* Precious
* Handmade
* Gift-worthy
* Emotional

Use:

* Close-up details
* Soft shadows
* Elegant backgrounds
* Lifestyle photography

---

## Competitive Position

Do not compete on price.

Compete on:

* Craftsmanship
* Personalization
* Emotional value
* Wedding memories
* Artistic quality
