# Ivory & Bloom Atelier Market Positioning

## Category

Luxury Handmade Bridal Accessories

---

## Avoid Being Seen As:

* Cheap wedding accessories
* Mass-produced gifts
* Generic Etsy shop
* Commodity products

---

## Desired Perception:

Ivory & Bloom Atelier should feel like:

"A boutique atelier creating timeless wedding treasures."

---

# Brand Differentiators

## 1. Handmade Story

Customers connect with the maker.

Show:

* Process videos
* Behind-the-scenes creation
* Artist journey

---

## 2. Personalization

Make every piece unique.

Examples:

* Names
* Wedding dates
* Colors
* Custom designs

---

## 3. Emotional Value

Position products as:

* Wedding memories
* Family keepsakes
* Future heirlooms

---

## 4. Premium Experience

Improve:

* Packaging
* Photography
* Website
* Customer communication

---

# Growth Direction

Build a brand ecosystem:

Wedding accessories →
Bridal gifts →
Wedding keepsakes →
Luxury personalized gifts
