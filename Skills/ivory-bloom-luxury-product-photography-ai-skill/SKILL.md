# AI Prompt Creation Formula

Every AI image prompt should include:

1. Subject

What is being photographed?

Example:

"handcrafted ivory satin bridal clutch"

---

2. Style

Example:

"luxury wedding editorial photography"

---

3. Environment

Example:

"elegant European wedding suite"

---

4. Lighting

Example:

"soft natural window light"

---

5. Camera Direction

Example:

"85mm lens, shallow depth of field"

---

6. Emotion

Example:

"romantic timeless bridal atmosphere"
