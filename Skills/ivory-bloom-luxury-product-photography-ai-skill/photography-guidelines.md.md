# Ivory & Bloom Atelier Photography Guidelines

## Product Photography Style

The goal:

Make handmade accessories feel like luxury heirlooms.

---

# Product Shots

Required:

## Main Image

* Clean composition
* Product clearly visible
* Premium background
* Strong visual impact

---

## Detail Shot

Show:

* Fabric texture
* Embroidery
* Personalized details
* Handmade craftsmanship

---

## Scale Shot

Show:

* Product in real wedding environment
* Realistic size
* Human interaction

---

# Wedding Editorial Style

Reference feeling:

Luxury bridal magazine.

Think:

* Vogue Weddings
* Elegant European weddings
* Fine art photography

---

# Models

Preferred:

Natural beauty.

Styling:

* Elegant bridal gown
* Soft makeup
* Romantic hairstyle
* Minimal jewelry

Avoid:

* Overly dramatic fashion poses
* Artificial expressions

---

# Backgrounds

Preferred:

* Luxury hotel suite
* Garden wedding venue
* European villa
* Romantic ceremony setting
* Elegant dressing room

---

# Materials

Highlight:

* Satin
* Silk
* Lace
* Pearls
* Flowers
* Gold details

---

# Image Consistency

Maintain:

Same:

* Lighting
* Color tone
* Luxury feeling
* Photography style

Across:

* Website
* Etsy
* Pinterest
* Instagram
