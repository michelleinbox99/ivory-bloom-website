# Ivory & Bloom Atelier — SEO Growth Strategy

Approved blueprint for long-term Google authority. Target: luxury personalized bridal
accessories. Status tags: **[LIVE]** · **[ENHANCE]** · **[GAP]**.

## 1. Keyword map

| Primary keyword | Target page | Secondary / long-tail cluster |
|---|---|---|
| personalized bridal accessories | `/` + `/collections/` | custom bridal accessories, made-to-order bridal accessories |
| handmade wedding gifts | `/collections/personalized-keepsakes.html` | handmade bridesmaid gifts, personalized wedding keepsake gifts |

**Intent tiers:** transactional → collection/product pages; informational long-tail → journal;
brand/trust → about/our-craft. **Local angle to claim:** "bridal accessories Northern Virginia /
Potomac Mills" (real place-history = low-competition local authority).

## 2. Page SEO strategy (per template)
- Home — owns "luxury personalized bridal accessories" + "wedding keepsakes" — **[LIVE]**
- Collection pages — title `<Category> | Ivory & Bloom Atelier — <benefit + made-to-order>`; **[GAP]** add ~300+ words unique indexable copy (short how-it's-made/how-to-order paragraph + contextual FAQ)
- About / Our Craft — brand + founder-experience terms; ProfilePage/Person **[LIVE]**
- Love Notes — "Ivory & Bloom reviews" + review rich results — **[LIVE]**
- All pages — unique title + meta + canonical — **[LIVE, verified]**

## 3. Blog (journal) — pillar & cluster
- **Pillar: Complete Guide to Bridal Party Accessories** ← existing coordinating/matching posts
- **Pillar: Wedding Color & Palette Guide** ← *Choosing Your Shade* + season palettes, "something blue" (pairs with email lead magnet)
- **Clusters to add:** flower girl gift ideas · bridesmaid proposal gifts · what are clay/everlasting flowers · wedding keepsakes that last · ring bearer pillow alternatives
- **Cadence:** 1–2 posts/month, each linking up to its pillar and out to its collection.

## 4. Internal linking (hub-and-spoke)
Home = authority hub → Collections / About-Craft / Journal / Real Weddings / Love Notes.
Rules: every journal post links to its relevant collection; every collection links to a
relevant journal post (**[LIVE]**) + a Real Wedding feature; About/Craft link to Collections;
Love Notes links out to collections. Descriptive keyword anchors, never "click here."

## 6. Image SEO
- **[LIVE]** descriptive alt text, width/height, srcset on key images
- **[GAP]** keyword-descriptive filenames for future/real photos (`pearl-handle-satin-bridal-pouch-dusty-rose.jpg`)
- **[GAP]** WebP/AVIF (tooling-blocked, specced) — CWV + image-search
- **[GAP]** image sitemap + figure captions
- Pinterest is a search engine for bridal — 2:3, descriptive images

## 7. E-E-A-T
- **Experience/Expertise [LIVE, major]** — founder story (13+ yrs, Potomac Mills, decades of design) + Person schema; the single biggest E-E-A-T asset
- **Authoritativeness [GAP]** — add author byline to journal posts (`author: Michelle`, linked to About Person); pursue press/directory listings; Etsy 4.9/246 anchors reputation
- **Trust [LIVE/ENHANCE]** — reviews + schema live; add per-collection reviews, a visible returns/care policy (owner decision), consistent NAP
- **Freshness** — lastmod live; blog cadence + Real Weddings keep the site active
