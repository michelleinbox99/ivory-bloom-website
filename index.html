Meet the Maker

A One-Woman Atelier, Built by Hand

Ivory & Bloom Atelier started at my own kitchen table — a place to bring twenty years of design experience into something you could actually hold. Every pouch, crown, and flower girl set that leaves this workroom passes through my hands, start to finish.

I believe your wedding accessories should feel considered, not generic. That's why every order is made after you purchase, in the colors and quantities your day actually calls for — not pulled off a shelf.
