Common Questions

What counts as a bespoke commission?

Anything that doesn't fit neatly into the standard collections — a piece combining elements from more than one category, an accessory reimagined for an unusual request, or an idea you haven't seen anywhere else. If you can describe it, it's worth asking about.

How is a bespoke piece priced?

By the design itself, since no two commissions are alike. Share your vision through the consultation form and you'll receive a personalized quote before any work begins. More questions →
