Common Questions

How do I size a flower girl crown without a fitting?

Have her parent measure head circumference with a soft tape just above the ears. The adjustable ribbon ties comfortably cover most girls between three and eight.

Should she carry a basket or a pouch?

We recommend the pearl-handle petal pouch: spill-proof, easy for small hands to hold, and a keepsake after the day rather than storage-closet clutter. More questions →
