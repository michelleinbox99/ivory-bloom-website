Common Questions

Can you match my exact wedding colors?

Yes — custom color matching is free. Send a photo of your dresses (natural light photographs work best) and the satin is matched to it, because "dusty rose" has to mean the same thing in your hands as it did on your screen.

What are the six signature shades?

Ivory, Blush, Dusty Rose, Lavender, Dusty Blue, and Sage. Golden-hour outdoor weddings flatter the warm shades, while bright coastal light and greenery-heavy venues flatter the cool ones. More questions →
