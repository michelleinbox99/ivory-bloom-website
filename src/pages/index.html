Meet the Maker

A One-Woman Atelier, Thirteen Years in the Making

I spent decades as a professional designer before I found clay flowers — and opened a small handmade shop at Potomac Mills Mall that slowly became this. Every pouch, crown, and flower girl set that leaves my workroom passes through my hands, start to finish.

A mother of four, I know how fast the day goes and how much a small kept thing can hold. That's why nothing here is pulled from a shelf — it's made for you, in your colors, to become part of the memory.

— Michelle, Founder

The Craft

Clay Flowers, Shaped Petal by Petal

Every piece is individually handcrafted to your wedding's colors and vision. Clay flowers are shaped and finished by hand so they never wilt. Satin is cut on the grain to catch the light in smooth bands. Pearl handles are strung and knotted like fine jewelry — because a keepsake should be built like one.
