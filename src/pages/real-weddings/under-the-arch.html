One Repeated Signature

Look closely at these frames and you'll see the principle every bridal party order at the atelier is built on: two or three neighboring hues spread across the party, the bride adjacent rather than above, and one detail repeated on every person to tell the eye these people are together.

Variation needs an anchor. Here it's the palette itself — champagne against ivory against blush — and in the pieces we make, it's almost always the hand-strung pearl handle: six different satin shades, one unmistakable signature.
